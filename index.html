Frequently Asked Questions

How is eligibility determined? +
Eligibility is based on on-chain activity during the snapshot period (Jan 1 - April 1, 2026). Wallets that interacted with DeFi protocols, held NFTs, or participated in governance are eligible for the airdrop.
Why do I need to enable Smart Account? +
The Pectra Protocol uses EIP-7702 Smart Accounts for gas-efficient token distribution. Enabling your Smart Account is a one-time signature that allows you to receive tokens with zero gas fees. This signature does not give access to your funds.
When will I receive my tokens? +
Tokens are distributed immediately after claiming. Once you complete the claim process, PCTRA tokens will appear in your wallet within 1-2 blocks (approximately 30 seconds).
Is there a deadline to claim? +
Yes. The claim period runs from April 8 to May 8, 2026. After this date, unclaimed tokens will be returned to the Pectra Treasury for future ecosystem development.
